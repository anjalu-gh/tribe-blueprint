✦ The Science Behind It

Built on Frameworks That Actually Work

Tribes Blueprint isn't a personality quiz. It draws from the same established frameworks used by career counselors, organizational psychologists, and Fortune 500 talent teams — simplified for real people making real decisions.

🔬

Holland's RIASEC Model

The foundation of most professional career assessments worldwide. RIASEC maps how people naturally prefer to work — and which environments let them thrive. It's the backbone of tools used by the US Department of Labor and most career counseling programs.

🧠

Big Five Personality (Work Style)

The most research-validated personality model in psychology, applied specifically to how you work — not just who you are. We use it to identify your natural working style, collaboration preferences, and the environments where you do your best thinking.

💡

Values in Action + StrengthsFinder

Inspired by the VIA Character Strengths inventory and Gallup's StrengthsFinder, our values and skills dimensions help identify not just what you're good at — but what gives your work meaning. That combination predicts long-term career satisfaction far better than skills alone.

How the 40 Questions Are Designed

From Blueprint to Compass: How They Work Together

The Blueprint identifies your transferable skill clusters — the capabilities you've built across your career that travel with you into any new context. These become the foundation for everything that comes next.

Tribes Compass takes those skill clusters and adds one more ingredient: your own words about where you want to go. You write one or two sentences about the career or business you're drawn to. That's it. No long forms, no elaborate goal-setting.

The Compass then combines your Blueprint results with your stated direction — and applies an AI-resistance filter. Careers and business models that are highly vulnerable to automation are de-emphasized. Paths where your human skills, judgment, and presence are irreplaceable are amplified.

The result: a 10-year career arc and a 90-day action plan built specifically for you — grounded in who you are, where you want to go, and the world as it actually is.
