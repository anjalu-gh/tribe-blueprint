What the assessment is built on

Drawn from the frameworks major corporations
have used for decades to understand their people.

Pathworks Blueprint draws on the foundational tenets of the assessments many major corporations have used for years to understand their employees:

DISC Myers-Briggs® (MBTI®) Big Five (OCEAN) CliftonStrengths® Holland Code (RIASEC)

We've distilled the most useful tenets of each into a single 7-minute, sliding-scale assessment that captures your work style, preferences, energizers, and natural interests. The result is your Pathworks Blueprint Profile — a clear snapshot of who you actually are at work. That Profile then becomes the foundation for the Pathworks Compass: add a few sentences about what you want to do next, and the system generates a personalized roadmap of AI-resistant careers, businesses you can start, a 90-day action plan, a 10-year arc, and the resources to help you get started.

Pathworks Blueprint is an independent assessment inspired by these conceptual frameworks. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or licensed from any of the publishers of these instruments. Myers-Briggs®, MBTI®, and CliftonStrengths® are registered trademarks of their respective owners.
