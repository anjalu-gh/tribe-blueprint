The Science Behind It

Built on proven frameworks — updated for the AI era

Pathworks isn't guesswork. The assessment draws from the most validated career science frameworks available, and adds the one dimension most assessments completely ignore: AI resistance.

Holland's RIASEC Model

The foundation of virtually every serious career assessment. RIASEC maps people across six work-style dimensions — Realistic, Investigative, Artistic, Social, Enterprising, and Conventional — to identify genuine career fit. Pathworks uses this as its core architecture.

Big Five Personality Applied to Work

The Big Five (OCEAN) is the most rigorously validated personality framework in psychological research. Rather than just labeling you, Pathworks applies it specifically to how you work — autonomy vs. collaboration, structure vs. flexibility, stability vs. change.

Transferable Skills Inventory

Inspired by StrengthsFinder and the Values in Action inventory, Pathworks adds a dimension most career tests miss: what you've actually built and done. Not just what you prefer — what you've proven. Sales, systems, coaching, creative work, leadership, technical expertise.

AI-Resistance Framework

Unique to Pathworks. A dedicated set of questions maps the five dimensions that AI genuinely cannot replace: physical presence, emotional attunement, high-trust relationship building, situational judgment in ambiguous human contexts, and deep community-level impact. This is what future-proofs your plan.

Future-Proof Your Career

Not all careers are created equal right now

The Compass doesn't just match you to careers you'd be good at — it explicitly steers you toward paths that AI will augment, not eliminate. Here's what that distinction looks like in practice.

⚠️ Higher AI Displacement Risk

✗ Roles built around routine data analysis or reporting
✗ Writing, editing, and content production at scale
✗ Scheduling, coordination, and administrative processing
✗ Basic customer service and tier-1 support
✗ Standard legal research, bookkeeping, and data entry

✅ Strong AI Resistance

✓ Roles requiring physical presence and hands-on judgment
✓ High-trust relationship and therapeutic work
✓ Complex human leadership and organizational navigation
✓ Community-embedded, place-based professional roles
✓ Entrepreneurial and creative work at the human frontier

The Compass maps your specific Human Edge scores against this framework — so you get recommendations matched to your unique AI-resistance profile, not generic advice.
